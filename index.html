About Me

Currently completing a fullstack software engineering bootcamp with the Flatiron School, I work full-time, remotely. Through coursework and projects, I am developing fluency in: React, Javascript, Node, Ruby, Rails, Sinatra, SQL, HTML, CSS, Rack, jQuery, and AJAX, as well as industry standards and best practices, while logging over 1000+ hours of coding time. I have several years of experience using R and SAS statistical modeling languages to build, analyze, and model large climate and environmental datasets as an ecologist/hydrologist. I understand the data behind the numbers- having collected, analyzed and modelled data for the last 10 years across scientific disciplines. Having relied on technology to analyze large datasets during this period, I started diving deeper into the 'ecology' and systems-design aspects of the software that I was using. Ultimately, I am a systems-thinker who enjoys solving problems with the best tools available, maintaining agility and improving my capacity and learning in both data science and software engineering.

Global Food Festivals CLI

This Ruby GEM provides the user a CLI to view "12 Global Food Festivals Worth the Trip" as described on Fodor's Travel website. This program will list all 12 festivals and the user can choose which festival, by number, they would like more information on. Details include a short description of the festival and the festival location.
